Our Mission

To democratize cybersecurity education and create a global community of skilled security professionals.

Founded in 2020, HACKLIDO began as a small group of passionate cybersecurity experts who recognized the growing skills gap in the industry. We envisioned a platform where anyone, regardless of their background, could access high-quality cybersecurity education and hands-on training.

Today, HACKLIDO serves over 1 million monthly visitors and has become a leading platform for cybersecurity learning, featuring thousands of challenges, tutorials, and a vibrant community of security enthusiasts.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

We believe quality cybersecurity education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation or educational background.

Community

Learning is more effective when done together. We foster a collaborative environment where knowledge sharing is encouraged.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform with cutting-edge technologies and teaching methodologies to stay ahead of threats.
